## Verdict

**RAC DOES NOT BEAT BASELINE**: Projected 63.6% vs 87.4% (-23.8%)

**Root causes of underperformance:**
1. Embedding top-10 recall is only 72.3% — correct answer not in candidates for 27.7% of items
2. BCIPL has BCIPL-specific items (Co., Deprn sheet, subnotes) not well represented in 6-company DB
3. Section mismatch noise: OCR section labels differ across companies, confusing retrieval
